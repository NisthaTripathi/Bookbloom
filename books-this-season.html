🌿 5 Must-Read Books This Season

← Back to Home

Looking for your next great read? Here are five hand-picked books for all kinds of readers—each one offering a unique journey through story, emotion, or thought.

  • 1. “Tomorrow, and Tomorrow, and Tomorrow” by Gabrielle Zevin
    A poignant tale of friendship and creativity, this novel explores the lives of game designers and their intertwined destinies.
  • 2. “The Midnight Library” by Matt Haig
    A thought-provoking look at the choices we make and the lives we could have lived. Perfect for anyone seeking meaning and perspective.
  • 3. “Ikigai” by Héctor García & Francesc Miralles
    A non-fiction gem from Japan that explores the concept of purpose and longevity, offering lessons that resonate across ages.
  • 4. “The Henna Artist” by Alka Joshi
    Set in 1950s India, this richly descriptive novel follows a woman navigating societal expectations and personal freedom.
  • 5. “Pachinko” by Min Jin Lee
    A sweeping generational saga set in Korea and Japan, exploring themes of identity, struggle, and resilience.

Whether you're in the mood for something introspective or immersive, these titles offer something for everyone. Happy reading!
