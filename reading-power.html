📚 The Power of Reading

Books are not just ink on paper. They're worlds waiting to be explored. In this post, we explore how reading daily can change your perspective, spark creativity, and improve mental health.

Reading strengthens your brain’s neural pathways, increases empathy, and enhances vocabulary and communication skills. It also reduces stress and aids in better sleep.

So if you’re ever feeling lost, open a book. It might just lead you back to yourself.
